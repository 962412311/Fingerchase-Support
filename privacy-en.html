Fingerchase Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Information We Process

Fingerchase stores your practice history, favorites, settings, imported scores, and conversion results on your device to provide practice, history restoration, and personalized features. Microphone practice, image/PDF score recognition, and audio transcription process relevant content only after you choose to use those features.

On-Device Processing and Storage

Practice analysis, personal scores, image/PDF recognition, and audio transcription are designed for on-device use. The app does not provide public links, sharing, export, or printing for personal scores, note data, or recordings.

Subscriptions and Payments

Apple processes subscription purchases, renewals, refunds, and payment information. Fingerchase does not receive your full card details or Apple Account payment credentials, but it uses subscription status supplied by Apple to determine whether Pro access is active.

System Permissions

Microphone access is requested only when you choose real-piano practice or calibration, and you may revoke it at any time in system settings. Images, PDFs, audio, and structured scores are processed only after you explicitly select them with the system file picker. The app does not continuously access your file library.

Retention and Deletion

On-device data remains until you delete the relevant content in the app or uninstall the app. Subscription expiration does not delete existing personal scores, permanently unlocked pieces, history, or settings.

Children's Privacy

Fingerchase does not require an account and does not collect children's personal information for advertising. Guardians should supervise a minor's device use and purchases as required by local law.

Updates and Contact

If our features or data practices materially change, we will update this policy and its effective date. For privacy questions, use our support page and do not include sensitive information.
