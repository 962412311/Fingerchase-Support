User Privacy Choices

For Fingerchase

Manage System Permissions

You can disable Fingerchase microphone access at any time in the system settings on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac. Images, PDFs, audio, and structured scores are processed only after you explicitly select them with the system file picker. The app does not continuously access your file library, so there is no separate file-library permission to disable.

Delete On-Device Content

You can delete personal scores and their related practice data, piano calibration data, and recognition-optimization data in the app. Other local history, favorites, and settings are removed with the app's data when you uninstall it. Confirm that you no longer need the content before uninstalling.

Manage Your Subscription

Apple manages subscriptions, renewals, cancellations, and refunds. You can view or cancel Fingerchase Pro in your Apple Account subscription settings. Deleting the app does not automatically cancel a subscription.

Accounts and Online Tracking

Fingerchase currently does not require an account, provide targeted advertising, or sell personal information. There are therefore no separate account profiling, advertising tracking, or data sale options to disable.

Contact Us

If you cannot complete a privacy choice using the options above, use our support page. Do not include passwords, payment information, personal scores, or recordings.
